Asian Glazed Chicken Thighs

Description

For my final recipe, I decided to go with my daughters' favorite dinner (that I cook at least). According to them, it is a deliciously crispy recipe that others should try cooking!

Ingredients

  • 8 skinless, boneless chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup of rice vinegar
  • 5 tablespoons of honey
  • 1/3 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/4 of taosted Asian sesame oil
  • 3 tablespoons of Asian chili garlic sauce
  • 3 tablespoons of minced garlic
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Whisk together honey, vinegar, soy sauce, sesame oil, chili garlic sauce, garlic, and salt in a bowl. Transfer marinade into a ziplock bag, followed by chicken thighs. Squeeze air out of the bag and seal tightly. Shake the bag a few times and the set bag in the refrigerator for an hour, turning the bag over at least once to well coat both sides of the chicken.

  2. Preheat oven to 425°F.

  3. Remove the chicken from the marinade bag and place into a 9x13-inch baking dish.

  4. Bake for 30 minutes, or until an instant-read thermometer reads 165°F. Let fully-cooked chicken stand for 5 to 10 minutes before plating with rice or your desired choice of sides!
